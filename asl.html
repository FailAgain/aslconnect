PST 301

ASL 1

ASL I is for students who have little or no previous knowledge of ASL. Students will learn basic conversational skills and vocabulary through video samples, exercises, and live signing practice with native users of ASL through video.

PST 302

ASL II

ASL II students refine their use of basic sentence types, and try out their skills through interactions, such as giving and receiving directions, with native signers through interactive video sessions with native ASL uers.

PST 303

ASL III

ASL III students will produce more complex ASL vocabulary and grammatical features, including the ability to tell and understand short stories, narratives, and dialogues. Students will put these skills to practice through live video sessions with native users of ASL through video.
